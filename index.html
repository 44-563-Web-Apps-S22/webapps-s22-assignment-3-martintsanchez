How to get to Walmart from KC airport?

  1. Take the ramp to US-71 S.
  2. Take the I-29 S/US-71 S exit toward US-59 S/Kansas City.
  3. Take exit 13 toward KCI Airport.
  4. Merge onto NW 120th St.
    1. At the traffic circle, take the 2nd exit onto Beirut Circle Entrance.
    2. Exit the traffic circle onto Beirut Circle Entrance.
  5. You get to your desired terminal.
